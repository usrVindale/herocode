What is Computer Engineering?

Computer Engineering (CpE) is a four-year engineering program that encompasses the science and technology of designing, developing, implementing, maintaining, and integrating software and hardware components within contemporary computing systems and computer-controlled equipment. This dynamic field immerses students in a comprehensive exploration of technology, equipping them with the expertise to bridge the virtual and physical worlds. CpE engineers are adept at crafting innovative solutions, propelling advancements in areas like artificial intelligence, robotics, and data science, and ensuring the seamless synergy between software and hardware, shaping the digital landscape of today and tomorrow.

What We do?!

As computer engineers, we specialize in designing and developing innovative technology solutions that power the modern world. Our work revolves around creating embedded systems, which are the brains behind everyday devices, ensuring they function efficiently and reliably. We excel in prototyping, bringing concepts to life through rapid experimentation and iterative development. Additionally, we expertly navigate the intricate world of networks, crafting robust communication infrastructures that enable seamless connectivity. Our proficiency in programming languages and software development empowers us to create software solutions that drive the functionality of hardware, delivering cutting-edge technology to enhance lives and businesses alike.


About our department

The Department of Computer Engineering declares its role to produce competitive engineers who are knowledgeable in the fundamental areas of digital and computer system design, software development, and modeling of physical systems.

The Department of Computer Engineering will impact the information age as a national leader in computing, research, and education.

After three to five years from graduation, the Computer Engineering alumni shall:

1. Have pursued advancement towards becoming globally competitive leaders in their chosen field of practice

2. Be a contributor to the development of a progressive society, guided by the UE core values of Excellence, Integrity, Professionalism, Teamwork, Commitment, Transparency, Accountability and Social Responsibility.

A. Apply knowledge of mathematics, science and engineering

B. Design and conduct experiments, as well as to analyze and interpret data

C. Design a system, component or processes to meet desired needs within realistic constraints

D. Function on multi-disciplinary teams

E. Identify, formulate and solve engineering problems

F. Have an understanding of professional and ethical responsibilities.

G. Communicate effectively in both Filipino and English languages

H. Have an understanding of the impact of engineering solutions in a global and societal context

I. Recognize the need for, and an ability to engage in life-long learning

J. Apply knowledge of contemporary issues



Student Organizations / RSO

Society of Computer Engineering Students | SCpES

The Society of Computer Engineering Students at the University of the East Manila is a registered student organization dedicated to fostering excellence and innovation within the field of computer engineering. Committed to enriching the academic journey, this vibrant society offers a diverse range of activities that cater to both curricular and extracurricular interests. From intense E-Games competitions that test gaming prowess to enlightening workshops and seminars that expand knowledge and skills, this organization provides a platform for computer engineering enthusiasts to connect, learn, and thrive. Join us in our mission to shape the future of technology!
